Fake Seed and Fertilizer Scams

Farmers are sold low-quality or counterfeit seeds, fertilizers, or pesticides labeled as premium brands.

Impact: Poor crop yield, financial loss, and long-term soil damage.

Loan Fraud / Fake Loan Agents

cammers pose as bank agents offering easy loans, collect documents and fees, and disappear.

Impact: Farmers lose personal data and money, and may become victims of identity theft.

Subsidy Scam

Middlemen or officials siphon off government subsidies meant for farmers.

Impact: Farmers do not receive the full subsidy or any benefit at all
